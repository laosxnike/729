↓ Common Questions
Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Membership is month-to-month. Cancel by emailing us — no questions, no pressure. Your benefits run through the end of the billing period.

Can I pause my membership?

Yes — up to three months per year. Useful if you're traveling, going through a hard season, or just need to step back. Email us when you need to pause.

Can two people share one membership?

Memberships are individual, but each tier includes guest passes. The Sangha gets two free guests each month, which is enough for a partner to come along regularly.

Is there a financial-need option?

Yes. We offer reduced-rate memberships funded by our Steward tier. Email us at hello@community729.com — quietly, no judgment, no forms.

Is membership required to come to classes?

Absolutely not. Most people who come here are not members. Membership exists for the people who come often and want to be in deeper relationship with the work. Not as a barrier — as an option.
