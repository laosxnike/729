06 Our Story
"Family-owned. Mother and son. Award-winning, but quiet about it. Open to every path, sincere about each one."

Community 729 was founded as a sanctuary in the heart of Orlando — a sacred space designed to support local artisans, honor both ancient and modern traditions, and foster real community among people walking their own spiritual paths.

Our vision is to cultivate an awakened world that transforms suffering into compassion and delusion into wisdom. The mission is simpler: hold the room.
