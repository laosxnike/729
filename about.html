06 Our Story

A small family. A sacred room.
Held since 2018.

Community 729 was founded as a sanctuary in the heart of Orlando — a space designed to support local artisans, honor both ancient and modern traditions, and foster real community among people walking their own spiritual paths.

"Family-owned. Mother and son. Award-winning, but quiet about it. Open to every path, sincere about each one."

Community 729 was founded as a sanctuary in the heart of Orlando — a sacred space designed to support local artisans, honor both ancient and modern traditions, and foster real community among people walking their own spiritual paths.

Our vision is to cultivate an awakened world that transforms suffering into compassion and delusion into wisdom. The mission is simpler: hold the room.

We opened the doors in 2018 with three classes a week, fifteen mats, and a singing bowl that had been in our family for forty years. The community found us. Practitioners we'd known for years started teaching here. The calendar filled. The marketplace grew. Nothing about it was strategic.
