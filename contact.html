↓ Frequently Asked

Things people often wonder.

Do I need to be experienced to come to a class?

No. Every class on our calendar welcomes complete beginners. If you're new to a practice, just arrive a few minutes early and tell the teacher — they'll make sure you have what you need.

Do I need to register, or can I drop in?

Most weekly classes welcome drop-ins — just arrive 10 minutes early. Retreats, ceremonies, and full moon gatherings require advance registration since space is limited.

What should I bring?

Yourself. We have mats, blankets, bolsters, and cushions for all classes. If you have your own, bring it. Wear something you can move and rest in.

Do you offer sliding-scale or scholarship pricing?

Yes. Most of our practitioners offer sliding-scale rates for private sessions. We also have a small scholarship fund for our retreats and longer programs. Just ask — quietly, no judgment.

Can I bring my child?

For most adult classes, no — we keep our adult sessions adult-only so the room can hold the energy it needs to. We do host occasional family meditation and yoga days, listed on the calendar.

Is the space LGBTQ+ welcoming?

Yes. Without qualification. Every practitioner here is committed to creating space that welcomes every body and every identity.

How do I become a practitioner here?

We don't have an open application — but we're always meeting new teachers. Come to a class first, attend a few sessions, get to know the room. Then write to us. We work slowly and we like to know who someone is before they teach.

Do you sell gift cards?

Yes. Digital gift cards in any amount, delivered by email, never expire. Find them in the shop.
