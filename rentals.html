↓ Each Room in Detail

What you can expect from each space.

Capacity, amenities, and what each room is best suited for. Every space comes equipped — bring yourself and your gathering.

Indoor · Capacity 24
Yoga · Meditation · Breathwork

East Room

Our flagship practice room. Bamboo floors, blackout curtains, sound-treated walls. Comes equipped with mats, blankets, bolsters, and a small altar. The room favors quiet practice.

  • 1,000 sq ft · 24 mat capacity
  • Sound-treated & soft lighting
  • Mats, blankets, bolsters provided
  • Bluetooth speaker system
$45/HR
Indoor · Capacity 30
Workshops · Talks · Ceremonies

West Room

Slightly larger and more flexible than the East Room. Wood floors, neutral walls, projection screen. Great for workshops, lectures, plant medicine ceremonies, and gathering circles.

  • 1,200 sq ft · 30 chair capacity
  • Projection screen + speaker system
  • Chairs & folding tables provided
  • Optional altar setup available
$55/HR
Outdoor · Capacity 40
Outdoor · Ceremonies · Ecstatic Dance

The Courtyard

A walled outdoor sanctuary with stone pavers, a fire pit, and string lights. Perfect for full moon ceremonies, ecstatic dance, cacao circles, and evening gatherings. Weather-dependent.

  • 1,500 sq ft · 40 person capacity
  • Fire pit (with permits) & string lighting
  • Outdoor speakers, weatherproofed
  • Backup indoor option included
$65/HR
